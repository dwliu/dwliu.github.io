It comes to an end

发表于 2009-03-11

Last morning I got the rejection letter from Yale, the university I have expected so much for the past half year…

Shuai, I wanna thank you for all you have done for me all through this period. I am sorry that I disappointed you, but I believe definitely that we will meet in United States sometime in the future. You have tought me a lot, and those are really great wealth for my futural life.

Things are getting clear for me. It seems at the beginning I had high self-evaluation for meself and for my application. Also I failed to recognize the severe situation caused by the economic downturn of United States. I admit right now that my application is almost failing. In most cases I will alternatively go to Hong Kong for a temporary employed position next month, and then try my best to stay there for a PhD degree when that emplyment ends.

Here I want to express my sincere thanks to all my friends. Shuai, Winnie, Lei, Wei, Qingzhen, and so many others that have helped me so much. I own you a lot, and I will never forget all of these memories.
